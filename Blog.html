This Is Us: Season 1

This Is Us Pilot introduces multiple people who shares the same birthday. The rest of the season continues to show how they are all connected.

This Is Us: Season 2

Season 2 continues to follow the Pearsons family as they all go through life struggles. This season also answers the question many fans have been asking since the first season - how did Jack die?
